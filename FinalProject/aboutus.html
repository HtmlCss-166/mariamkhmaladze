About Vintage Store

We are so much more than the average vintage clothing company. We are a family run business who are just as passionate about making you happy as we our about the beautiful unique vintage and retro pieces we sell. We are dedicated to our business and we always provide good old fashioned customer service that's genuine and personal to you. No call centres, no automated robots, nothing faceless, we are here to help you at anytime. We are advocates for positive body image and we want you to feel great in anything you buy from us. If you aren't sure what will suit you or need a little help with styling, just ask! We pride ourselves on the level of service we provide to ensure that each and every one of our customers can have access to our expertise. If you require any help or advice you can call us, send an email, contact us via social media or call into our vintage shop and we will always be happy to help with no cost or obligation to buy. Both our online vintage emporium and our vintage shop in Darwen, Lancashire are filled to the rafters with amazing pieces of unique vintage clothing, designer vintage, 50s clothing, indie clothing, vintage accessories, vintage costume jewellery, vintage collectables and vintage homeware. But we don't stop there. We also stock a wide a varied range of alternative clothing, brand new vintage retro and reproduction pieces including accessories, jewellery, retro clothing, fifties style and vintage style rockabilly dresses. Our stock literally changes on a daily basis, with new items added to the website and put out in the shop constantly.
